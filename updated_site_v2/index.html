Why Choose Me

7+ years of experience: I have guided more than 500 students worldwide across IB, IGCSE, GCSE, AP, A‑Level and CBSE boards. Learners from grade 5 through 12 consistently show remarkable growth in understanding, confidence and grades.

One‑to‑one & personalised: All sessions are strictly one‑to‑one, customised to each learner’s needs, pace and goals. I adjust lesson plans, pacing and even textbook selections based on the parent’s and student’s requirements.

Interactive & hands‑on: Using a digital writing tablet and annotation tools, students write, solve and explain directly on my screen. I provide real‑time feedback and use simulations and online tools to make abstract concepts tangible.
